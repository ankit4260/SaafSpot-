The process

Five steps, start to finish

Every report follows the same path — so points stay fair and the leaderboard stays honest.

01

Spot it

Find a dirty spot and drop a pin on the map — no typing addresses from memory.

02

Show proof

Upload a before photo. Cleaning it yourself? Add a video and an after photo too.

03

Get checked

Image and location checks plus community review decide a trust score.

04

Earn points

Verified gets full points, partial gets half, no staged photo gets none.

05

Redeem & rise

Climb the leaderboard and unlock cashback, vouchers and badges.
